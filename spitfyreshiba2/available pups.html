SPITFYRE SHIBA








Every shiba inu puppy is raised lovingly with my children from birth. This produces a very sound, gentle temperament and a much better family companion. This shows in the puppies we raise, as well as the resulting adult shiba inu. We do not believe in sacrificing anything in the process. Our days are spent interacting with our shiba inu, grooming, walking and playing (or even napping). I know the key to a rich relationship with any shiba inu is very dependent on good breeding and socialization. Every shiba inu is bred for excellent conformation, health, and temperament to become the best family companion available. In hopes that they will meet every expectation you have of them.

Pictures tend to make the pups look a lot bigger then they actually are. Keep in mind an adult shiba is only 14-15 inches high and about 18-30 pounds.
